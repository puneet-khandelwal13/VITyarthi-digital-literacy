Responsible Social Media Use – Checklist

Do’s
Use respectful and polite language in all posts and comments
Think before posting—ensure content reflects your values and personality
Protect your privacy by adjusting account settings and limiting personal information
Share accurate and verified information to avoid spreading misinformation
Maintain a professional image, especially on platforms used for academics or careers

Don’ts
Do not post offensive, abusive, or inappropriate content
Avoid sharing fake news or unverified information
Do not overshare personal details like phone number, address, or passwords
Avoid engaging in online arguments or cyberbullying
Do not post anything that could harm your reputation or future opportunities
