Stay Safe Online – Checklist

Prevention Tips
Use strong, unique passwords for every account (combine letters, numbers, symbols)
Enable two-factor authentication (2FA) on important apps (email, banking, social media)
Never share OTP, PIN, or passwords with anyone—even if they claim to be from a bank
Avoid clicking on unknown links or downloading suspicious attachments
Always verify website URLs (look for https:// and correct spelling)
Keep your devices updated with the latest software and security patches
Limit personal information shared on social media (phone number, address, ID details)
Log out from accounts when using public or shared computers


Financial / UPI Safety (Important)
Never approve unknown UPI payment requests—receiving money does NOT require approval
Double-check the UPI ID and name before sending money
Avoid scanning random QR codes shared by strangers
Use only official apps like Google Pay, PhonePe, or Paytm

Report Cybercrime Immediately
Report online fraud at the National Cyber Crime Portal: cybercrime.gov.in
Call the Cyber Crime Helpline: 1930 for immediate assistance
